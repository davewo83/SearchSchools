Welcome to School Finder

Whether you are moving into a new area or wondering if your child will get into the same school as his/her friends, this site is for you.

By entering your postcode below, you will get a list of all the schools in your area. More importantly, you will get the distance from your postcode to the school and whether you would have got into those schools for the past 3 years.

Currently this site only supports Dudley schools.
